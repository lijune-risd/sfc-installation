Ceremony of Movements

Student Fitness Center Installation at RISD

Aim

The installation attempts to be that working partner; however, it changes one important factor. Rather than words being verbally spoken as motivator boosts, the installation provides that vigor through visual text that is motion tracked to your skin color. Hence, as one moves, one sees the phrases that motivate themselves to move more.

Student Fitness Center

As a personal space, the Student Fitness Center at RISD allows one for meditation. The space can be contextualized as a motivated space — a property specific to the fitness realm. However, ever so often, students who utilize the space need an extra push. An extra push-up, another sit up, or another minute of plank – these elements can be hard to practice without another working partner.
